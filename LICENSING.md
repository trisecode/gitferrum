## FAQ

**Can I use GitFerrum for free?**
Yes. The open source edition under AGPL-3.0 is completely free for personal and commercial use, as long as you comply with the AGPL-3.0 terms.

**Can I build a product on top of GitFerrum?**
Yes, but your product must also be AGPL-3.0 licensed and you must share its source code. If you want to keep your product proprietary, you need a commercial license.

**Can I fork GitFerrum?**
Yes. Forks must comply with AGPL-3.0 (open source, same license, source available).

**What is the difference between the free and premium editions?**
The free edition includes the full Git client core. The premium edition adds advanced features for professional and team use (details at https://gitferrum.com).
